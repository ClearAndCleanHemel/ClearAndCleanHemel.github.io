About Clear and Clean

Three people, one standard

Clear and Clean is a small, focused team based around Hemel Hempstead, specialising in private car and window cleaning. No franchises, no rotating crews — just three people who know exactly how they like things to look when they’re finished.

The work is intentionally local and intentionally personal. Every appointment is handled by Miguel, Abdul or Francisco themselves, so the standard stays consistent from one visit to the next.

Payment is simple: you pay on completion of the service, once you’re happy with the result. Bookings are confirmed manually so that every slot is realistic and honoured.
